Powering Tamil Nadu with Affordable Green Energy

Save 20–30% on your electricity bills with Raygen’s Rural Solar + Captive Model

The Problem

Commercial businesses in urban Tamil Nadu face extremely high electricity tariffs of ₹11+ per unit. Monthly bills often exceed ₹1 lakh for supermarkets, salons, and textile shops. This directly reduces profitability and growth. Rooftop solar adoption in urban areas is very limited due to lack of space, high costs, and strict regulations. Meanwhile, rural Tamil Nadu has abundant sunlight and large stretches of unused land that can generate low-cost renewable energy but is currently underutilized. The disconnect between rural solar potential and urban demand creates both a challenge and an opportunity.

Our Solution

Rural Solar Farms

We strategically set up solar farms in rural Tamil Nadu, where land is cheaper and sunlight is abundant, ensuring maximum energy generation at lower costs.

Direct Grid Supply

Using the TANGEDCO grid and Open Access regulations, the energy generated in rural solar farms is directly supplied to urban commercial clients, bypassing the constraints of rooftop solar.

Affordable Pricing

We provide electricity at a flat ₹7–8 per unit, 20–30% cheaper than EB rates. Clients pay only for what they consume with zero upfront cost, simple UPI/GPay billing, and complete transparency.

How It Works: The Group Captive Model

The Group Captive Model, as per the Electricity Act 2003, allows multiple clients to collectively own a share in a solar project and consume the power generated. Here’s how it works with Raygen:

1. Equity Ownership

Each client owns at least 26% equity in the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) created for the solar farm, ensuring partnership and accountability.

2. Guaranteed Consumption

Clients commit to consuming at least 51% of the generated power annually, fulfilling the regulatory requirement for captive power plants.

3. Energy Delivery

Raygen supplies the power through the TANGEDCO grid using Open Access, ensuring reliable delivery without extra infrastructure for the client.

4. Transparent Billing

Clients are billed at a flat, predictable tariff via UPI/GPay with access to a live tracking app that monitors consumption, savings, and environmental impact.
